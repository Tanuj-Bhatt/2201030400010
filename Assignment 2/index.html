About Us

Neighbourhood Skill Exchange is a focused platform located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We are dedicated to connecting people with valuable skills and knowledge through a collaborative exchange system. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where people can learn, grow, and thrive together. Join us in fostering a culture of sharing and empowerment among the youth in our neighbourhood.
